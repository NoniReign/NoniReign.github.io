Future Use

It’s certainly easier and more exciting to talk about the possibilities rather than the challenges when it comes to drones. So, what’s coming down the line? As drone technology advances, it will be possible to both up and down scale current uses. On one hand this means potential for wearable drones – quite literally personal assistants that travel around with us. On the other end of the scale it means drones replacing traditional infrastructure such as cars and commercial freight carriage vehicles. What is a certainty is that the drone industry will certainly become more valuable over the next decade. How fast this happens and what the drones of tomorrow look like is an entirely different proposition – it’s up to industry leaders and beneficiaries to shape the future of the industry by innovating responsibly.
